## P3 — Rate limiting on audit-log export endpoint

**What:** Add per-firm rate limiting to `GET /api/audit-log` when used with `format=nacha` (the NACHA export path added in the sprint). Currently authenticated but no throttle — a compromised firm account could spam the endpoint and pollute the AuditLog with `nacha_export` self-log entries.

**Why:** At 10 clients, not a real threat. At 100+ clients, log pollution degrades the audit trail value and the endpoint could be used to exhaust the 10k row cap with synthetic export records.

**Effort:** S (human: ~1h / CC: ~10min)

**Where to start:** Add a Redis- or KV-based rate limiter in `app/api/audit-log/route.ts` gated on `format=nacha`. Max 10 exports per firmId per minute. Use Vercel KV or Upstash.
