## 1. Campaign Overview

**Campaign Name:** "Stop It Before It Leaves" — Vantirs Market Launch

**Summary:** A 4-week, zero-budget product launch campaign to establish Vantirs as the go-to pre-payment fraud detection platform for mid-market finance teams, drive demo bookings, and build foundational SEO presence.

**Primary Objective:** Book 15–25 qualified demo calls with CFOs, Controllers, and AP Directors at mid-market companies ($10M–$500M revenue) within 4 weeks of launch.

**Secondary Objectives:**

- Build a LinkedIn following of 200+ finance professionals
- Generate 50+ website visits per week by end of Week 4
- Validate messaging and ICP fit through reply-rate data

## 2. Target Audience

### Primary Segment: CFOs & Controllers at Mid-Market Companies
- **Company size:** $10M–$500M revenue, 50–2,000 employees
- **Industries:** Manufacturing, logistics, healthcare, construction, professional services, real estate
- **Pain points:** Manual invoice verification, fear of BEC (business email compromise) scams, growing vendor base with no automated payment controls, audit and compliance pressure
- **Motivations:** Protecting the company from financial loss, reducing personal liability, passing audits, modernizing AP workflows without massive IT projects
- **Where they spend time:** LinkedIn, CFO peer communities (CFO Alliance, Proformative), industry conferences, Google search when a fraud scare happens
- **Buying stage:** Problem-aware but solution-unaware — most know invoice fraud is a risk but don't know pre-payment verification tools exist

### Secondary Segment: AP Directors & Finance VPs
- More operationally focused — care about workflow integration, time savings, and reducing manual review burden
- Likely the internal champion who brings Vantirs to the CFO for budget approval
- Active on LinkedIn and in AP/finance operations communities

### Audience Profile
> "A CFO or Controller at a $50M–$300M company in manufacturing or healthcare who processes 500+ invoices per month and has either experienced a payment fraud attempt or is nervous about one. They discover solutions through Google searches after a scare, LinkedIn content from peers, or cold outreach that references their specific industry. They care most about: (1) stopping fraud before money moves, (2) no heavy IT lift, and (3) proving ROI to their board."

## 4. Channel Strategy

Given a $0 budget, this plan relies entirely on owned and earned channels, leveraging the assets you've already built (100 personalized emails, 500 ICP leads, SEO strategy).

### Channel 1: Cold Email (Primary Conversion Channel)
- **Why:** You have 100 personalized emails written and 500 ICP leads ready. Cold email is the fastest path to demo calls at zero cost.
- **Format:** Personalized 1:1 emails, 7-touch sequences over 21 days, A/B tested subject lines
- **Effort:** Medium (daily sending discipline, reply management)
- **Expected output:** 15 emails/day → 75/week → 300 over 4 weeks. At a 3–5% positive reply rate, that's 9–15 meetings booked from email alone.

### Channel 2: Content/SEO (Foundational — Long-Term Payoff)
- **Why:** Zero indexed pages today. Every blog post published and indexed is a permanent lead generation asset. Your SEO strategy already has content briefs written.
- **Format:** Long-form blog posts (1,400–1,800 words) targeting commercial-intent keywords; homepage messaging overhaul
- **Effort:** High (2–3 hours per post to write, optimize, and publish)
- **Expected output:** 6–8 published posts over 4 weeks. First indexation within 1–2 weeks. Long-tail traffic begins by Week 3–4.

### Channel 3: LinkedIn Organic (Awareness + Credibility)
- **Why:** Your ICP lives on LinkedIn. Every post builds credibility that makes cold emails more effective (prospects check your profile before replying).
- **Format:** Personal posts from your founder account — fraud stats, industry insights, behind-the-scenes of building Vantirs, hot takes on AP process failures
- **Effort:** Low-Medium (15–30 min/day for posting and engagement)
- **Expected output:** 5 posts/week. Goal: 50+ impressions per post by Week 2, 200+ by Week 4.

### Channel 4: LinkedIn Direct Outreach (Supplementary)
- **Why:** Multi-channel touches increase cold email reply rates by 2–3x. A LinkedIn connection request on Day 2 of an email sequence creates familiarity.
- **Format:** Personalized connection requests tied to email sequences; comment engagement on ICP posts
- **Effort:** Low (5–10 connection requests/day paired with email sends)

### Channel 5: Website/CRO (Conversion Infrastructure)
- **Why:** Every channel above drives traffic to vantirs.com. If the homepage doesn't convert, everything upstream is wasted.
- **Format:** Homepage headline rewrite, trust bar, CTA optimization, demo booking flow
- **Effort:** Medium (one-time sprint in Week 1, then ongoing iteration)

## 9. Risks and Mitigations

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|
| **Email deliverability issues** — domain gets flagged as spam due to cold outreach volume or missing authentication records | Medium | High (kills your #1 channel) | Verify SPF/DKIM/DMARC before Day 1. Cap at 15 emails/day in Week 1. Monitor bounce rates daily. If bounce rate exceeds 5%, pause and investigate. Consider Instantly.co warmup. |
| **Low reply rates** — messaging doesn't resonate, ICP targeting is off, or emails land in spam | Medium | Medium | A/B test subject lines from Week 1. Analyze open rates vs. reply rates separately (low opens = deliverability problem; high opens + low replies = messaging problem). Iterate copy weekly based on data. |
| **Content takes longer than planned** — writing 2 blog posts per week while sending emails and managing replies is ambitious for a solo founder | High | Medium | Prioritize email outreach over content if time is tight. Blog posts #6–8 are nice-to-haves. Focus on the 4 must-have posts. Consider using AI to draft first versions, then edit for voice and accuracy. |
| **Website doesn't convert** — homepage messaging update doesn't move the needle on demo bookings | Low-Medium | High | Track demo page visits vs. bookings separately. If traffic exists but bookings are flat, the page needs work. Add an ungated 3-min demo video as a secondary CTA. Test "Free Payment Audit" as an alternative offer. |
